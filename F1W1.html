Welcome to Beagle World!

Beagles are a breed of small hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare.

Fun Trivia about Beagles

  1. Origin: Beagles originated in England.
  2. Size: Beagles typically weigh between 20-25 pounds.
  3. Temperament: Beagles are known for their friendly and curious nature.
  4. Popularity: Beagles are one of the most popular dog breeds in the United States.
